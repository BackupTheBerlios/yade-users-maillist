Anton Gladky said:     (by the date of Mon, 20 Apr 2009 08:38:19 +0200)

> What is your opinion, is it really difficult to learn YADE for adding new
> modules?

I guess that the difficulty in adding new modules is roughly equal to
the difficulty in understanding the "MyTetrahedron" example.

Find all the files that contain "MyTetrahedron", eg. with this command:

  grep MyTetrahedron -l --include="*.hpp" --include="*.cpp" ./* -R

And read the comments inside. Look at the Publications on our website too.
