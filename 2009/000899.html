you need to put a [[Category:Developer guide]] inside a wiki page,
and it will be put by the wiki engine automatically into this
category. Just look at how other pages are written.

This "Content page on wiki" is in fact the "Browse YADE" expandable
tree on the fron page, just click it, and you will see all the
categories and articles.

"All" means all those that have a proper [[Category:SOMETHING]]
written inside them.
