Hello all yade users, and happy new year. :)

I just (r1605) commited a fix for a bug introduced recently (kn was not 
registered with interaction physics, I still don't know when it was 
introduced exactly).
If you see a strange behaviour when reloading a scene, consider updating 
your version.
The bug is invisible if you just run a TriaxialTest from scratch (no 
interaction in the initial state), but you can't miss it if you 
run/save/reload.
If you didn't notice anything wrong, don't worry : you probably didn't 
download the wrong code. This is not the sort of bug you would not notice...
